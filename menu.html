Menu

At Mesiagareh Ramen, we offer a diverse menu that caters to all ramen lovers. Our carefully curated selection features classic favorites like Tonkotsu, Shoyu, and Miso ramen, alongside creative options such as Curry ramen. Each dish is crafted with rich, flavorful broths, tender noodles, and a variety of premium toppings. You can also customize your bowl with extra ingredients like soft-boiled eggs, chashu pork, or vegetables to make your meal uniquely yours. Whether you're craving something traditional or adventurous, our menu promises a satisfying experience with every bite!

Miso Ramen

Miso ramen is a hearty Japanese noodle soup characterized by its rich, savory broth made from miso paste, which adds a deep umami flavor. It is typically served with toppings such as sliced pork, green onions, bamboo shoots, and a soft-boiled egg, offering a satisfying and warming meal.

Shio (Salt) Ramen

Shio ramen features a light, clear broth flavored with salt, creating a delicate and refreshing taste. The dish is typically accompanied by toppings like tender pork, seaweed, green onions, and a soft-boiled egg, offering a balanced and savory experience.

Shoyu (Soy-sauce) Ramen

Shoyu ramen is a flavorful Japanese noodle soup with a broth made from soy sauce, giving it a savory, slightly tangy taste. It is often garnished with toppings like sliced pork, green onions, bamboo shoots, and a soft-boiled egg, creating a rich and satisfying dish.

Tonkotsu (Pork) Ramen

Tonkotsu ramen features a rich, creamy broth made by simmering pork bones for hours, resulting in a deep, savory flavor. It is typically served with toppings such as tender chashu pork, green onions, mushrooms, and a soft-boiled egg, offering a hearty and indulgent meal.

Curry Ramen

Curry ramen combines the bold, spiced flavors of Japanese curry with the comforting warmth of a noodle soup. The rich, thick curry broth is usually served with toppings like sliced pork, vegetables, green onions, and a soft-boiled egg, creating a hearty and flavorful dish.s
